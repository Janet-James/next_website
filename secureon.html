Services

Infrastructure Management Service encompasses the management of components such as policies, processes, equipment, and data for overall effectiveness. It can be categorized as System Management, Network Management, and Storage Management. The System Management would take care of ensuring that the System (both physical and Virtual) are highly available for computation. Network Management would take care of providing a network without unnecessary bottleneck and high throughput. Storage Management would take care of providing abundant storage space and high availability

The Infrastructure Management Service would be having complete control over the Infrastructure, ensuring that the business services are with zero downtime.

The Infrastructure Management Service would also ensure that the necessary backups are done.

Following are the benefits that can be leveraged from having an Infrastructure Management Service.

  • Ensure the adherence to standards
  • Reduce the effort duplication
  • Enable an adaptable environment
  • Reliability of the Operation Environment
  • Guaranteed Service Availability
  • Superior Service Level Agreement
  • Sustainability of the Application Life cycle
  • The development team can focus on code and not worry about the infrastructure
