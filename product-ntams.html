NEXT Total Asset Management System – NTAMS

Assets are the predominant component of any organization. To add the values of the organization, managing assets is crucial. Allocating assets and monitoring the value and what form the value depends on the organization and its stakeholders. It’s arduous for the organizations to keep track of their assets like what condition they are in, or where they are located. The growth of the business is the top priority and keeping track of their assets in digital format becomes more important in operating day to day business. Asset management is the process of maximizing the assets of the organization to provide the best returns to stakeholders. Businesses have a wide range of assets and it is important to manage their assets and use them to get the maximum possible result. Asset management is based on four fundamentals: Value, Alignment, Leadership and Assurance with focus across the whole asset life cycle and the approach to decision making.

NTAMS - An Integrated Digital Enterprise Solution

NTAMS is an integrated digital enterprise solution empowered with GIS, for managing both fixed mobile assets that are used to record and track an asset throughout its life cycle, from procurement to disposal. NTAMS provides information like where assets are located, who is using them, how they are being utilized and details about the assets. This system provides users with the ability to monitor the asset usage and status of compliance and enables them to identify problematic areas of asset management. NTAMS is highly configurable and secured which enables companies to lower administrative burdens relating to the tracking, organizing, and monitoring of assets across the locations and useful to track, collaborate and report on the entire asset lifecycle.

Asset Maintenance

At various intervals, all assets require some level of maintenance like identifying assets, assets location, assets condition, etc. Scheduling and tracking their maintenance provides the true cost and revenue associated with assets.
