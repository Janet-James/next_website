Powering Education with Solar Energy Benefits

Benefits to the Students

Lightening & Extended Study hours

With our lighting system, it has the provision of lighting which enables longer studying or classroom hours at schools in all levels. It will also facilitate both improved quantity and quality of studying.

Facilitation of Digital

Digitally enabled systems improves student achievement, access to schooling, increase efficiencies and reduce costs, enhance students’ ability to learn and promote their lifelong learning, and prepare them for a globally competitive workforce.

Benefits to Teachers

Enhanced staff retention and teacher training

It enhances their learning experience, staff retention and lead to better teacher training. It provides teachers with better training, new skills and techniques for improved practices in the classroom.

Better school performance

The benefits of electricity-based lighting, digital, and improved teaching lead to better school performance, higher enrolments rates, improves graduation, completion rates, and the achievement of higher test scores.

Benefits to Environment

Total Greenhouse gas reduction

Solar power reduces the detrimental impact on the environment. It’s a clean energy, unlike oil and coal, solar doesn’t spew toxic chemicals into the atmosphere or add greenhouse gas emissions. It diminishes the need for fossil fuels, which contribute to a reduction of Greenhouse gas emissions.

Carbon footprint reduction

Mitigates the fossil fuels, shrink the carbon footprint. Ensures the healthcare sectors in reduction of more than 15,000 pounds every year. Increase in Environmental upgrade

Benefits to the School Authority & Sustainability

Low maintenance

Solar PV systems require very low maintenance. Durable and last around 25-30 years with no maintenance. Solar panel system requires keeping clean, the inverter and cables also need maintenance to ensure solar power system runs at maximum efficiency. Less expense on maintenance and repair work.

Sustainable Development:

GREEN with the Sustainable living solutions and Powering Education with Solar energy can make a significant contribution to ensure access to affordable, reliable and modern energy services. It enriches zero pollute, harm the soil or air, minimize the production of greenhouse gases, avoids noise pollution, contributes to Sustainable Development Goals.

Benefits in Economic Context

Solar Power Ensure a Bright Future

Solar power systems have the power to set schools free from the energy companies constantly rising electric rates. In addition, it has the ability to improve school productivity and ROI.

Cost Savings

With the reduced and predictable electricity rates offered to schools, the considerable amount will have saved, especially GREEN solar systems protects from unstable utility costs.

Benefits to the Society

Control over Electricity & Peak Power

Greater impact in reduction in power bills. Solar panels are a great resource to draw from to avoid brownouts and blackouts by utilities. Utilities can use the panels to generate extra energy, enabling them to solve the peak demand problems that plaguing the country.

Enablement of community co-benefits

School electrification produces multiplier effects such as better community health enablement or sanitation, gender equality and also decreases migration and increase resilience.
