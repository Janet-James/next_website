Captive Power Plant for Corporate

A Captive Power Plant is a plant that supplies power wholly or primarily to Hotel industrial customer rather than to a utility. It ensures security of power supply, coupled with greater control of increase or decrease in generating capacity, to align with production cycle. It operates in grid parallel mode with the ability of power generation by Corporates for its exclusive consumption and provides energy efficient managementto reduce energy consumption.

Powering Corporates with Captive Power Plant ensures energy efficiencies without compromising the standard customer services. The energy efficiency saves energy costs, decrease emissions, enhance the government’s service delivery capability and improve the productivity of the staff. It empowers Corporates to achieve sustainability at low cost for operations and preserve the quality of environment.
