Energy Problem – Energy Access and Resilience

1.2 billion worldwide are living in the dark and hundreds of millions more live without inadequate or unreliable access. A significant proportion of rural areas with electricity are vulnerable to an unreliable electricity, long outages and fluctuations. In rural areas the power outages are more frequent in rural than in urban areas. It results in increased use of conventional sources of resources which have health and environmental repercussions. It’s hampered the socioeconomic development linked to inadequate healthcare, low literacy rates, and low levels of productivity etc. Rural enterprises located in villages are not able to function at maximum efficiency. This leads to poor economic returns and rural people migrate to cities. Energy is a core element for Rural Development.
