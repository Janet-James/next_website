NEXT Land Management System – NLMS

Land plays a pre-eminent role in our existence and sustenance. All of us are associated with Land in one way or the other. Land Parcel differs in size and varies from living in our house and ranges from simple farming to complex usage like mining industries etc. The land is partitioned accordingly. The cadastre is a mechanism for recording comprehensive land ownership. It ranges from an individual to the government. Land ownership is recorded as cadastre. Managing Land records is crucial. Providing Land-based services and accurate information to the landowners as well as planners is a herculean task. Having land parcel data in a digital form not only allows for easier access and use of the data but also to share the data with associated government bodies. Managing Land Information consists of providing an accurate, current and reliable land record cadastre and its associated attribute & spatial data that represent the legal boundaries of land tenure. Land Information is necessary for many public programs ranging from land planning, infrastructure development, and maintenance, environmental protection and resource management, emergency services, to community & social service programs. Land Information is the basis for land markets, regional development, and other economic activity.

Vision

To enable Land-based Services with multi-functional and self-service enabled.

Mission

To provide an easy-to-use and convenient Land Administration Engine that can provide open access to high quality and up-to-date Land information and spatially enabled Land Management services.

Objectives

To promote, facilitate, coordinate and support the development of a dynamic and flexible geospatial environment that will:

  • Ensure Legislative compliance
  • Establish, facilitate and maintain a transparent land Registration practice in consultation with Relevant Stakeholders
  • Ensure Standard format & maintenance of tiling system
  • Ensure title information is correctly recorded on the title deed & Registration database
  • Ensure all lodgement & dealings are attended to promptly
  • Ensure Legal titles are only issued to Rightful Lessees
  • Improve Data recording and title records system
NLMS – AN ENTERPRISE SOLUTION FOR LANDS
  • NLMS is an integrated Digital Enterprise Solution empowered with GIS for managing Land Information
  • Bringing a mature & holistic approach to Land Information Management, LMS is an all-inclusive solution designed to manage the Nation's most important asset - Land cadastre
  • Built over the geospatial platform, NLMS facilitate tools and modules over a friendly interface
  • NEXT Land Management System has pioneered to build a digital solution exclusively for managing the land related services
NLMS – FUNCTIONS
Land Information

Land Information includes Parcel Details, Ownership details, Title deed details, Tax & valuation details. Our Identification Tool facilitates the user to know the tool specific Attributes/details on the location where the user clicked. NLMS provides a tool to get details of land if land details available at the clicked location.

Land Search

Land Search permits to find for a particular Land and fetch the details on the map. The auto-complete feature of the Land search shows the possible matches based on the user entry which gives an insight to the user about the available Lands. Search by land fetches the information with layer based and user based constraints.

Land Services

Land service request tool is for the user-friendly feature using which one can raise for Land-related services or bill payments which will connect to respective departments to get a solution for the service request. Service requests can be requested for Land Subdivision, Land Valuation, Land title deed & Tax payment.

Land Measurement

Land Measurement Provides basic mensuration like the length of a line & the area of a polygon over the map. Before or after performing each measurement, units of measure can be changed in the context menu. Land Measurements are also shown on the map on just clicking on the particular land.

BI & Spatial Reports

Various Reports are generated in the NCPS and can be grouped as follows:

  • Land Area Reports
  • Land Assessments Reports
  • Revenue Reports
  • Governance Reports
  • Year wise Reports
  • Characteristics Reports
Land Title Deed

Land Title Deed is a formal document which serves as evidence of ownership. This feature can provide the latest available title deed from the land register which can be verified for and related transactions & services. The Landowner can download the latest title deed.
