NEXT City Profiling System - NCPS

As the world continues to move forward towards urbanization, the need for effective city profile planning continues to develop. Urban planning becomes more intricate as time goes by because of the existing infrastructures. City profiling is used to analyze the urban growth and its direction of expansion, and to find suitable sites for further urban development. City Profiling is an accelerated and action-oriented urban assessment of needs and capacity-building. Providing civic services and a decent quality of life to residents. Having centralized & shareable data from associated government bodies in digital form not only allows effortless access and use of the data but also enables analytics to the decision-makers. Managing City data includes providing an accurate, current and reliable metrics and its associated attribute & spatial data that represent the legal boundaries of the urban landscape. City Profiling is an essential tool for quantifying urban landscape into various metrics ranging from urban planning, infrastructure development, and maintenance, resource management, emergency services, to community & social service programs. City Profiling is the key to real estate, urban redevelopment, and other economic activity.

NCPS – An Enterprise Solution For Civic Services

A digital innovation for visualizing real-time developments & managing proactively the city services with a cost-effective solution to the stakeholders. Plays a pivotal role in City planning, building, and development, asset management, infrastructure design and renewal, fire dispatching, capital project planning, budgeting, identifying infrastructure locations, municipal elections, property assessments, and much more. Built over a geospatial platform, NCPS facilitate Business tools and modules over a friendly interface. NEXT City Profiling System has pioneered to build a digital solution exclusively for managing the civic services.

Vision

To enable provision for basic Civic Services with cross-functional and self-service enabled.

Mission

To provide an easy-to-use and smart solution that can provide open access to high quality and up-to-date Utility information and spatially enabled Civic services.

Objectives

  • Real-time civic information & Mapping Urban Assets
  • Location-based data visualization & sharing
  • Effective monitoring & Efficient decision support
  • Better planning for providing optimum civic services
  • Request for new services as well as issues in the existing services
  • Ease of issuing Building permits & reporting Violations
  • Improved BI based Reports & Spatial Analytics.
Land, Building & Utility Information

Our Identification Tool facilitates the user to know the tool specific Attributes/details on the location where the user clicked. City Profiling provides three types of feature identifications: Land identification – To get details of land if land details available at the clicked location Building identification – To get details of the building if building details available at the clicked location. Utility identification - To get details of utilities if utilities detail available at the clicked location

Incident Reporting

The purpose of the incident report is to find the exact details of the occurrence of the utility where the issue happened. This information may be useful in the future when dealing with liability issues stemming from the incident. Combining these incidents and mapping out their geographic attributesallows one to visualize the spatial visualization of the incidents happened.

Building Permit

Building Permit is formal permission to begin the construction, demolition, addition or renovation on your property. As part of the Building Permit process, Issuing Authority must review your plans to ensure they comply with the National Building Code, local Zoning By-laws and other Applicable Law. The city requires permits to ensure safe building, effective land use, effective water supply, Electricity planning, and high-quality site planning. Building regulation enforcement is applicable to the new building. Existing building permits to ensure change land use, building alteration and building demolishing. It shows utility services details and sends a service request.

BI & Spatial Reports

Various Reports are generated in the NCPS and can be grouped as follows:

  • Building Reports
  • Land Reports
  • Service Request Reports
  • Revenue Reports
  • Violation Reports
  • Incident Reports
Building Violations

Building code that has rules for the conditions in buildings. Building Violations is recorded in the system and shown. Building violation, when a hazardous or non-hazardous condition occurs in a real estate property, without a permit, issued by a municipal agency, such as a Department of Buildings.

Land & Building Search

Our Land & Building Search permits to find for a particular Land or Building and fetch their details on the map. The auto-complete feature of the building search shows the possible matches based on the user entry which gives an insight to the user about the available features.

Service Request

The Service Request can be elevated for basic utilities such as water, electricity, sewerage, telecom, etc. The service request facilitates to view the available utility services, raise new service request and log service problems.
